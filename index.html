About Us

Welcome to SAA

Association of Success in Action SAA is a non -profit organization which was registered as local NGO in 20 Dec 2023. It has General Assembly (GA) at the apex. Under its GA, there is board of director then office secretariat that leads the overall activities of the organization. SAA has been established by five founding members’ initiative with taking the leading part by Mr. Mohammed Ahmed to implement sustainable community development programs. Hence, SAA is emerged towards making interventions on issues pertinent to climate change, livelihood, Water Sanitation and Hygiene (WASH), Health, DRR , timely response to emergency and to ensure sustainable resilience to any of unexpected hazards..
